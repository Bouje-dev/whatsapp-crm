Data Deletion Request

According to Facebook Platform rules, we provide a way for users to request their data deletion.

How to delete your data?

If you wish to remove your account and all associated data from Waselytics, you can do so by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your Waselytics account.
  2. Go to Settings > Profile.
  3. Click on the "Delete Account" button at the bottom of the page.

Alternatively, you can submit a manual deletion request:

  1. Send an email to admin@waselytics.com.
  2. Subject: "Data Deletion Request".
  3. Please include your registered email address.

* We will process your request and permanently delete your data within 30 days.
