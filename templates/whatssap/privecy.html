Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Disound we collects, uses, and protects information when users access our application or use services integrated with the WhatsApp Cloud API (Meta Platforms, Inc.).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, phone number, and WhatsApp Business Account ID when users authenticate using Facebook Login or authorize the WhatsApp API.
  • Message metadata: Timestamps and delivery status may be stored temporarily for performance, analytics, and debugging. We do not store or share message content.
  • Device & technical data: IP address, browser type, and operating system for security and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate users securely via Facebook Login.
  • Enable and manage WhatsApp communication using the Cloud API.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent user data. We may share information only in the following limited cases:

  • With Meta Platforms, Inc. to operate the WhatsApp Cloud API.
  • With trusted service providers who assist with hosting or security (under strict confidentiality agreements).
  • When required by law or government authorities.

4. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services or to comply with legal obligations. Message content is never permanently stored on our servers.

5. Your Rights

Users have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of their personal data. You can withdraw consent by disconnecting your WhatsApp Business Account or Facebook Login.
